Register

Revamped


Registration fee: Rs.200/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 5000


Members per team:3 (2 designers + 1 model)


Mandatory requirements: Personal make-up for the model, stilettos


Details:


Round 1: Quiz


The participants will be given 25 questions based on fashion trivia.
Out of 25 there will be 5 star questions.
In the case of a tie, the team that has answered the maximum number of star questions correctly progresses to the next round.
The winner of this round receives immunity in the next round.


Round 2: Tasks


Each team will be given 2 tasks to complete in 30 minutes.
Round 1 and Round 2 scores will be added and the top 7 teams will progress to the final round.
In case of ties, there will be a tiebreaker task.


Round 3: Styling


The teams will be given 1 theme.
Participants must style their model's hair and make-up according to the theme.
The participants have to be very creative and can also modify their dress from Round 2 to suit the theme as they please.
Teams will be provided with a time period of 35 minutes to dress-up their model.
The final creation will be modeled in front of a panel of judges. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
