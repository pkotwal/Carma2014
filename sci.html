The Science of Deduction


Registration fee: Rs.50/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 3000


Members per team: 2


Details:


“There is nothing more deceptive than an obvious fact.”
-Sir Arthur Conan Doyle



Round 1:

o A part of a question will be shown to the participant. If answered correctly immediately, the participant will be awarded 10 points.

o Hints will progressively be given for the same. After every hint, the number of points the participant can gain from answering that question correctly will keep on decrementing.



Round 2:

o This will be a riddle round in which a sheet will be provided to the participants with various logical, intellectual and mathematical questions. The questions must be answered correctly.

o Hints for a particular question can be obtained by trading answering time. Further details will be revealed at the time of the event.



Round 3:

o This will be the final round in which a crime scene will be created. The teams will have to play the part of a detective to solve the mystery.

o The primary object will be to try and unravel the murderer based on the crime scene evidence and the suspect profiles provided.

o The teams will have time to examine the crime scene and discuss all the data acquired.
