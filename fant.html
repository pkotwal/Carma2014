Here's to the commitment, the ecstasy, the pain, the teamwork, the passing, the breathtaking strikes, the gravity-defying saves, the speed, the tackles, the honor, the trust, winning, losing, holding on and wanting more.
Here's to more than just a game, here's to a way of life. Here's to football.
Sign up as a team and battle it out on the pitch in rounds that test your knowledge, skill, tactics and strategies.


Round 1:


The 1st round will be a football quiz, during the course of which money for Round 2 can be won. The quiz will have 4 sets of questions, based on varying levels of difficulty.

Level 1 (Difficulty : Easy)
10 questions, each correct answer worth a cash value of 20 million.
Level 2 (Difficulty : Normal)
5 questions, each correct answer worth a cash value of 50 million.
Level 3
3 MCQ questions, each correct answer worth a cash value of 50 million.
Level 4 (Difficulty : Hard)
2 questions. If attempted correctly, the contestant receives 100 million. If attempted incorrectly, a penalty of 50 million will be deducted. The winner of the question round receives an advantage, which will be disclosed at the end of Round 1.
