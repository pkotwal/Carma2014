Stockmania


Registration fee: Rs.50/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 7000


Members per team: 2


Details:


Participants will follow a virtual stock market. Each team will be provided with a computer,
using which they will be able to buy and sell stocks.

At the start of the event, participants will be briefed on how the stock market works and about the various factors that affect the market.

At the start of the event, each team will be provided with Rs. 10 lac,
which can be used to purchase stock. Each round will constitute one quarter of a year.

During each round, participants will be provided with certain pieces of news relat
ed to their stocks.
They will have to decide whether to buy, sell or hold on to their stock.
At the end of each round, new stock prices will be displayed.

Under certain circumstances, the teams will be able to mortgage their stocks and under other circumstances,
the merging of teams will be allowed. The winner will be decided based on net valuation (both money and stock).
