The Amazing Race


Registration fee: Rs.150/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 12000


Members per team: 3-4


Details:




Day 1:

o All teams start from the same checkpoint.

o The team of 4 will be split into two of 2 each and will be given separate clues, which will lead towards the same destination in the end.

o The clues of all members will be checked in the end to ensure they have visited all the checkpoints.

o At each checkpoint they will receive a task to complete or a clue that will lead to the next checkpoint.

o The first 15 teams will qualify for the next round.





Day 2:

o The day will start from the point where Day 1 ended.

o The participants will be working in their original teams of 4.

o They will receive 2 cards(Red and Green) at every checkpoint from which they will have to follow any one.

o The red card will contain a tougher clue that will directly lead to one point.

o The green card will contain an easier clue that will lead them to another point where they will receive yet another easy clue that leads to the same point as the red card.

o The will be judged on how quickly they reach the final destination.
