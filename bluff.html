Bluffmaster


Registration fee: Rs. 30/-


Prizes: Rs. 3000


Details:


-The contestants are asked a few questions and they are supposed to answer with their opinion. If their answers match our list of answers (from the survey), they win the points allotted for that answer.

-Contestants need to pick a sheet from a number of sheets kept inverted on the table. One of the sheets is the con sheet (the sheet with all the answers).

-The sheets for each contestant will have one question per page and the con sheet would have one question followed by all its answers per page.

-The quizmaster asks the first question to Contestant 1. After he has answered successfully or unsuccessfully, Contestant 2 answers. And so on, till the last contestant is questioned.

-The second question starts with the first answer from Contestant 2 and then the rest follow in order.

-The order of the contestants will be selected via chit draw.

-After every 2 questions, an elimination will take place, in which one contestant nominated by the rest as the con, will be eliminated.

-If they are not successful in identifying the con, they lose half their points and then the game continues in the above fashion.

-If they do successfully eliminate the con, they all return their set of sheets and pick a new set of sheets again and then proceed in a similar fashion.

-After only 4 contestants are remaining, there will be no con and every contestant will have to answer on self.

-Each point they win equals the amount they earn. (e.g. 127 points = Rs. 127)

-In case of a tie during eliminations, a buzzer round is held.

-The contestant with the most points in the end, wins.
