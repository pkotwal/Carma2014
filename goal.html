Goal Manager


Registration fee: Rs.400/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 12000


Members per team: 6


Details:


o Each match will be of 5-6 minutes.

o 4 players will begin the match and the other 2 will be rolling substitutes.

o Players are not supposed to use their hands to save shots (there is no goalkeeper).

o Conceding 3 corners will result in a penalty.

o If the result of the match is not decided in normal time, penalties will be taken by 3 players present on the field.

o The decision of the Match Referee will be final and binding.
