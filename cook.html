Cook It to Win It


Registration fee: Rs.100/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 15000


Details:



Round 1:


Part A: Taste Test
All participants will be given the same dish to taste.
The dish will feature approximately 30 ingredients.
The contestants will be given a maximum of 10 minutes to taste and identify as many ingredients as possible.
For every correct ingredient they get awarded one point.

Part B: Guessing Round
All the participants will be shown 15 different food ingredients using a projector, which they must recognize and note down. Every correct answer results in a +1.
The top 25 contestants will progress to Round 2.
In the case of ties, the scores awarded in Part A will be compared.


Round 2:


Contestants will have to prepare a dish with the ingredients given to them in 30 minutes.
The top 5 contestants will be selected from the batch based on the following 4 criteria:

o Taste

o Innovation

o Technique

o Presentation
