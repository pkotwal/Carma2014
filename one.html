One-Hour Job


Registration fee: Rs.100/- per team


Prizes: Rs. 7000


Members per team: 2-3


Details:


“Why spend ten dollars to buy one item that does two things, when for five dollars apiece I can sell you two items that each does one thing?”
–Jarod Kintz


Come witness what it’s really like to be a salesperson and you might just find yourself enjoying it.
It's a challenge that will test your marketing skills and see one lucky team emerge the winner!


Round 1:

Each team will be given an object. The teams have to sell their objects for the maximum amount. This task has to be completed in the first 30 minutes. Each team has to earn a minimum of Rs. 100 to qualify for the next round.



Round 2:

Certain objects will be auctioned in this round. The teams have to buy these objects with the remaining money from Round 1. A maximum of 5 objects can be bought by a single team. Teams have to sell these objects for the maximum amount in the remaining 30 minutes. Whichever team earns the maximum money wins the event.
