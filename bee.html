Spelling Bee


Registration fee: Rs.50/-


Prizes: Rs. 3000


Details:


Round 1: Selecting the right spelling
Participants will be shown placards with 4 different spellings of a word.
3 will be misspelled and they have to pick the correct spelling.
The placards will be shown for 20 seconds and the answering time will be 5 seconds.
This will be a timed test with negative marking (+2,-1).

Round 2: Selecting the right word
This will be in a fill-in-the-blanks format.
The round will feature words which share similar basic meanings but have subtly different nuances or connotations.
Participants will have to choose the correct word out of a set of close synonyms to fill in the blanks.
This will be a timed test with negative marking (+3,-1).

Round 3: Common Confusable
Participants’ knowledge of homophones, homonyms and homographs will be tested.
The format will be the same as round two.

Round 4: One-word substitutes
This will be an oral round.
Participants will be given a phrase, which they have to substitute with a word.
There will be liberal time limits, though incorrect answers will result in elimination.

Round 5: Thematic technical vocabulary round
This will also be an oral round.
Participants will have to provide meaning to technical words which conventional dictionaries or thesauruses don’t usually include.
There will be liberal time limits, though incorrect answers will result in elimination.

Round 6: Buzzer round Spell Bee


The final two will be given 20 words in a rapid-fire format. The contestant thatspells the most words correctly, wins.
o Unnecessary banter in the last three oral rounds must be avoided.
o The participants must maintain decorum, especially during the written rounds.
o Spellings for round 5 will be taken from the Merriam-Webster Dictionary.
o The words can be of origins other than English.
o In case of conflict, the organising team’s decision will be final.
